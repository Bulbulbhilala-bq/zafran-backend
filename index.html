Our Story

Rooted in Heritage,
Refined by Passion

Zafran was born from a simple belief — that exceptional food is the most honest form of hospitality. Our chefs blend time-honoured recipes with contemporary technique to deliver flavours that are both familiar and surprising.

We source directly from local farms and artisan producers, ensuring every ingredient on your plate carries meaning and quality.
